Mexico Market Brief

Latin America's bridge between cash economies and digital entertainment innovation

Market Overview

Mexico represents Latin America's second-largest online entertainment market, characterized by a unique blend of traditional cash-based commerce and rapid digital adoption. With over 100 million internet users and growing smartphone penetration, the market is at an inflection point where digital entertainment consumption is accelerating across demographics.

The country's proximity to the US market, combined with a strong domestic entertainment culture, creates a distinctive competitive environment where international operators and local champions coexist. Established brands like Caliente and newer entrants like Codere compete across multiple verticals.

SEGOB & Regulatory Framework

Mexico's online entertainment regulation falls under SEGOB (Secretariat of the Interior), which administers the federal licensing framework. The regulatory approach has matured significantly in recent years, with clearer guidelines for online operations complementing the long-established land-based licensing structure.

State-level variations add complexity — while federal law provides the overarching framework, individual states maintain influence over local operations. A comprehensive comparison of Mexico's regulatory structure alongside four other Latin American jurisdictions is available in the Mexico regulatory analysis.

Payment Infrastructure: Cash Meets Digital

Mexico's payment landscape is defined by the coexistence of cash and digital. OXXO, with 21,000+ convenience stores, serves as the country's de facto cash-in network — enabling consumers to fund digital accounts using physical cash. This infrastructure is critical for entertainment platforms seeking to reach beyond the fully banked population.

On the digital side, CoDi (Cobro Digital) and SPEI (Sistema de Pagos Electrónicos Interbancarios) provide real-time electronic payment capabilities. The interplay between these systems creates a payment ecosystem unlike any other market we cover.
