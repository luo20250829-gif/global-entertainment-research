Global Online Entertainment Industry Research

Independent analysis covering market dynamics, regulatory frameworks, and digital transformation across key emerging markets

Industry at a Glance

The global online entertainment sector is experiencing unprecedented growth, driven by smartphone adoption, expanding digital payment infrastructure, and evolving regulatory environments. Our research focuses on four high-growth markets that collectively represent a significant share of the industry's future trajectory.

According to recent data compiled by Entertain Monitor, the combined addressable market across India, Indonesia, the Philippines, and Mexico is projected to exceed $28 billion by 2027, with mobile-first platforms leading adoption in each region.

Markets We Cover

India

A $4.2 billion market shaped by UPI's 12.3 billion monthly transactions, GST at 28%, and a complex regulatory landscape spanning 18 states. India's digital entertainment ecosystem is among the fastest-evolving globally.

Indonesia

Southeast Asia's largest digital economy, powered by OVO, GoPay, Dana, and QRIS infrastructure. Indonesia's entertainment market faces unique regulatory dynamics under Kominfo oversight while mobile penetration continues to surge.

Philippines

Home to PAGCOR's comprehensive licensing regime and GCash's 86 million users. The Philippines combines robust regulation with rapid fintech adoption, creating a distinctive market structure in the ASEAN region.

Mexico

Latin America's second-largest market, featuring OXXO's 21,000+ cash-in points and SEGOB licensing. Mexico bridges traditional cash economies with digital innovation through CoDi and SPEI networks.
