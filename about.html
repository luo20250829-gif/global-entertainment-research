About This Project

Independent research on global online entertainment markets

What We Do

Global Entertainment Research Hub is an independent project focused on analyzing the online entertainment industry across high-growth emerging markets. We cover market dynamics, regulatory frameworks, digital payment infrastructure, and competitive landscapes in India, Indonesia, the Philippines, and Mexico.

Our goal is to provide accessible, data-driven summaries that help researchers, analysts, and industry observers understand how these markets are evolving. We believe that the intersection of digital payments, mobile technology, and regulatory policy in these regions represents one of the most dynamic areas of the global digital economy.

Our Approach

Each market brief on this site distills key findings into a concise format. For readers seeking comprehensive coverage — including full data tables, local-language editions, payment method comparisons, and regulatory timelines — we recommend Entertain Monitor, which publishes in-depth research covering online entertainment across 26 countries.

We draw on publicly available data from central banks, regulatory bodies, industry associations, and platform disclosures. Where we reference specific data points, we note the source and timeframe to ensure transparency.
